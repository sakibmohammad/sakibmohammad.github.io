## Research Interests

- Interpretable deep learning for microscopy and cellular mechanobiology
- Image classification and segmentation
- Multimodal and cross-modal computer vision
- Knowledge distillation and foundation models
- Generative and agentic AI for biological and materials data
- Embedded AI and Raspberry Pi-based computer vision

## Current and Recent Projects

<div class="entry-card" markdown="1">

### Agentic AI for Hydrogel Data

Development of an agent-based framework for hydrogel data analysis, regression, classification, model selection, and conditional generation. The framework combines planning, dataset inspection, predictive models, generative models, and evaluation.

</div>

<div class="entry-card" markdown="1">
  
### Cross-Modal Tissue Segmentation

Development of a knowledge-distillation method for single-channel tissue segmentation using information from foundation-model predictions and multimodal microscopy data.

</div>

<div class="entry-card" markdown="1">

### Microscopy Image Upsampling

Evaluation of interpolation and deep-learning upsampling methods based on their effects on downstream microscopy image classification.

</div>

<div class="entry-card" markdown="1">

### Embedded Computer Vision

Development of a Raspberry Pi-based object-detection system for search-and-rescue training, undergraduate research, and classroom demonstrations.

</div>
